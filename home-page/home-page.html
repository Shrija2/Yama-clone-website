Our Story

Yama Vans is the manifestation of our desire to defy convention and live lives of our own invention. And we knew we weren’t alone. Now we make vans for people like us. People who want to write their stories on the roads beneath their wheels, the trails beneath their boots, the waves beneath their boards, and the powder beneath their skis.

More about us

Your adventure starts here. Yama Vans is based in Calgary, Canada, delivering custom vans across North America. We build vehicles for adventure that allow you to spend more time living freely.
